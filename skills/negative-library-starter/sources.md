# Sources & basis

This skill is the public version of TNT Growth's negative-library-build methodology — generating a categorized negative keyword library from business and industry context, before search-term data exists.

## The categories and approach
The category taxonomy, match-type defaults (phrase-first), shared-vs-campaign-specific split, and the "don't over-block / cross-check against target keywords" discipline are TNT's standard negative-build practice. They're operating heuristics, not universal laws — the skill tunes categories by vertical.

## Relationship to search-term-scorer
Two different jobs:
- **negative-library-starter** (this skill) — *proactive*. Build a starter library from the business when there's no data yet.
- **search-term-scorer** — *reactive*. Score a real search terms report 1-10 and surface negatives from actual queries.

A new account uses this skill first, then switches to search-term-scorer once the search terms report has enough volume to mine.

## What this skill deliberately does not claim
- No specific client names or client results.
- No guaranteed spend savings — value depends on how much irrelevant traffic the account would otherwise attract.
- It builds *candidate* negatives for human review, not an auto-posted list.
